## What to include

<Card title="Send to: frequency528ironworks@gmail.com" icon="envelope" href="mailto:frequency528ironworks@gmail.com?subject=Custom%20Iron%20Design%20Request">
  Click to open a pre-filled email
</Card>

Please include:

- **Photos of the area** where the iron work will go (front door, gate, railing, window, etc.) — clear, well-lit, taken straight on
- **A blueprint or sketch** if you have one (optional)
- **Measurements** — height, width, and any other relevant dimensions
- **Style preferences** — modern, traditional, ornate, simple, etc.
- **Reference images** of designs you like (optional but helpful)
- **Your name and phone number** so we can follow up
- **Your location** (city/state) for shipping or install info

## Photo tips

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Take photos straight-on">
    Stand directly in front of the area, not at an angle. This helps us create an accurate overlay.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Use good lighting">
    Daylight works best. Avoid heavy shadows or backlighting.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Include the full area">
    Make sure the entire space where the iron work will go is visible in the frame.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Send the original file size">
    Don't resize or crop too much — higher resolution gives us more to work with.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
